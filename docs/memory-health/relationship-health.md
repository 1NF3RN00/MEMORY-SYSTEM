# Relationship Health Specification

## Overview

The middleware evaluates:
relationship quality continuously.

Relationship health affects:

* traversal quality
* reranking quality
* semantic continuity
* graph expansion precision

Relationship quality is:
measurable infrastructure quality.

---

# Core Philosophy

Relationships are:
living semantic infrastructure.

Relationships naturally:
strengthen,
decay,
or destabilize over time.

---

# Relationship Health Signals

Examples:

* traversal success
* reranking contribution
* reinforcement history
* semantic coherence
* temporal relevance

Relationship evaluation remains:
deterministic and observable.

---

# Health Recovery

Recovery systems may trigger:

* relationship reweighting
* pruning
* semantic relinking
* adjacency reevaluation

Recovery remains:
controlled and measurable.

---

# Relationship Philosophy

Strong semantic relationships improve:
long-term retrieval quality.
