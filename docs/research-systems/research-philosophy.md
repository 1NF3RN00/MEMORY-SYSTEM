# Research Systems Philosophy Specification

## Overview

The middleware supports:
advanced semantic infrastructure research systems.

Research systems extend:
core deterministic middleware infrastructure.

Research systems remain:
secondary to:
stable semantic infrastructure foundations.

---

# Core Philosophy

The middleware prioritizes:
deterministic infrastructure FIRST.

Adaptive intelligence systems are layered:
on top of:
stable governed infrastructure.

The middleware avoids:
premature autonomous complexity.

---

# Research Objectives

Research systems attempt to improve:

* retrieval precision
* graph quality
* orchestration quality
* semantic density
* infrastructure scalability

Research systems remain:
observable and measurable.

---

# Governance

Research systems must remain:

* governed
* explainable
* reversible
* tenant-isolated

The middleware prioritizes:
controlled adaptive infrastructure evolution.

---

# Final Principle

Advanced intelligence systems should:
enhance deterministic infrastructure,
not replace it.

The middleware values:
stable semantic infrastructure first,
adaptive intelligence second.
