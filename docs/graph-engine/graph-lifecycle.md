# Graph Lifecycle Specification

## Overview

The semantic graph evolves over time.

Relationships may:

* strengthen
* decay
* archive
* prune
* reinforce

The graph is:
living semantic infrastructure.

---

# Core Philosophy

Graph quality evolves through:
retrieval and interaction.

The middleware continuously manages:
semantic graph quality.

---

# Graph Lifecycle

```txt id="gl1"
Relationship Creation
↓
Traversal
↓
Reinforcement
↓
Weight Evolution
↓
Decay
↓
Pruning
↓
Archival
```

---

# Reinforcement

Successful traversal may increase:

* relationship strength
* traversal weighting
* retrieval priority

Graph reinforcement improves:
semantic continuity.

---

# Decay

Unused or weak relationships may:
gradually decay.

Decay prevents:
graph pollution and traversal noise.

---

# Graph Philosophy

The middleware treats:
graph quality
as critical semantic infrastructure quality.

The middleware prioritizes:
high-quality semantic connectivity.
