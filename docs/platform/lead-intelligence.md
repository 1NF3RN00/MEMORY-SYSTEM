# Lead Intelligence Specification

## Overview

The platform supports:
semantic lead intelligence systems.

Lead intelligence evaluates:

* pricing interest
* urgency
* engagement quality
* booking signals
* escalation likelihood

Lead intelligence improves:
business workflow orchestration.

---

# Core Philosophy

Lead systems should remain:
contextual and analytics-driven.

The middleware avoids:
spam-oriented automation behavior.

---

# Lead Intelligence Signals

Examples:

* repeated pricing queries
* high urgency
* repeat engagement
* workflow progression
* booking intent

Lead intelligence remains:
observable and configurable.

---

# Lead Philosophy

The platform values:
high-quality contextual lead orchestration.
