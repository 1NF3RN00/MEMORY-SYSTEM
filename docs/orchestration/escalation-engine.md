# Escalation Engine Specification

## Overview

The middleware supports:
deterministic escalation handling.

Escalation occurs when:

* confidence is low
* retrieval fails
* user frustration increases
* governance restrictions apply
* human intervention is preferable

Escalation is:
workflow infrastructure.

---

# Core Philosophy

The middleware recognizes:
not all problems should be solved autonomously.

Escalation protects:

* retrieval quality
* user trust
* governance
* operational reliability

---

# Escalation Signals

Escalation may consider:

* low confidence
* repeated retries
* frustration signals
* ambiguity
* restricted access

Escalation remains:
configurable and observable.

---

# Escalation Philosophy

The middleware values:
honest orchestration behavior
over fabricated certainty.
